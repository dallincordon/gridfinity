## Base

A [post on Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/gridfinity/comments/1qnl1ch/about_to_set_up_and_print_my_first_base_have_a/) had the following suggestions in response to the question, "How can I determine how many bases I need to make sure \[the base] fits snug and doesn’t slide around?"

> just plug the internal dimensions of your drawer into GRIPs:
>https://makerworld.com/en/models/704997-grips-gridfinity-interlocking-baseplate-system#profileId-634873
>it will generate you the optimum baseplate

>Input your drawer dimensions in my tool [extrabold.tools](https://extrabold.tools/). Select your 3d printer and it cuts the grid so it fits your printer
